## Design Context

### Users
Hiring managers, recruiters, and data leaders evaluating Jacob Cuthbertson's BI/analytics expertise. Viewed on desktop during working hours and on mobile during commutes. They scan quickly — credibility and clarity matter most.

### Brand Personality
Warm, precise, sharp. Confident but approachable. Data-driven yet human. Not corporate or sterile — more editorial magazine than enterprise SaaS.

### Aesthetic Direction
- **Theme**: Warm dark — not cold blue SaaS. Inspired by the day-to-night coastal landscape scroll transition.
- **Color**: Warm amber/gold accent against deep warm navy. All neutrals tinted toward brand hue. No cyan, no purple gradients.
- **Typography**: Epilogue (display, sharp geometric) + Figtree (body, warm readable). Strong weight contrast.
- **Motion**: Scroll-driven background crossfade (day → sunset → night) as the primary effect. Subtle fade-up reveals. No bounce, no elastic. Everything decelerates smoothly.
- **Anti-references**: Generic dark SaaS landing pages, glassmorphism card walls, gradient text, neon-on-dark.
- **References**: Editorial magazine layouts, Dribbble shot 26366091 (clean social media management platform design — generous space, clear hierarchy, soft rounded elements).

### Design Principles
1. **Content earns its space** — generous whitespace, no filler decoration
2. **Time tells a story** — the scroll-driven day-to-night transition is the hero moment
3. **Warm precision** — data expertise communicated through warm amber tones and sharp typography, not cold tech aesthetics
4. **One bold thing per section** — each section has one clear focal point, no visual competition
5. **Invisible craft** — motion and transitions feel natural, never performative
